About Budget Tracker

Learn more about how Budget Tracker can help you manage your finances.

Budget Tracker is a simple web application designed to help you manage your finances. You can add income and expense transactions, filter them, and view your financial summary.

Features:

  • Add and delete transactions
  • Filter by income or expense
  • View total income, expenses, and balance
